Chenxin Li

I am a Ph.D. student at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Yixuan Yuan. I received my M.Eng from Xiamen University under Prof. Xinghao Ding and Prof. Yue Huang, where I also earned my B.Eng.

My research ranges from building large visual foundation, reconstruction and generation models to now unifying them as a callable toolkit for multi-modal intelligent agents. Currently, I am devoted to tightening this synergy by advancing multi-modal spatial reasoning ability for complex real-world challenges. Welcome to chat for potential collaboration and internship opportunities.
